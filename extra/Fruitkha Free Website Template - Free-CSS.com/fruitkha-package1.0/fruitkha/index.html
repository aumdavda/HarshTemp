Finding Newer & Intelligent ways to care for the world.

What we do?
Manentia Advisory provides medical imaging analysis and detection solutions using latest technologies. For preclinical and clinical labs users to assist their image analysis tasks as well as workflows with powerful deep learning and machine learning.
Who Are We?
Manentia Advisory provides medical imaging analysis and detection solutions using latest technologies. For preclinical and clinical labs users to assist their image analysis tasks as well as workflows with powerful deep learning and machine learning.
Company Work
Manentia Advisory speeds up the workflow of radiologists and pathologists and increasing the accuracy of diagnosis by helping doctors identify the disease and its stage of infection at an earlier stage.
Impact of AI on
Medical Diagnosis
Using AI helps reduce delays in identifying the abnormal medical images. Over 90% healthcare data comes from medical imaging from which 97% are not analyzed. AI can help by rapidly analyzing images and data registries and achieving a better understanding of patients conditions.

Things we offer to save your precious time

From empowering researchers in the identification of novel biomarkers of disease, and supporting R&D scientists to helping pathologists enhance the accuracy of diagnostics.

Hippa

Our company provides HIPAA-based compliance with cloud-based storage systems for medical data security. It means that hospitals or physicians can easily access their data anywhere.

Report Generation

Quick and accurate diagnosis reports so that doctors can spend more time on patient treatments.

Expeditious Results

Detailed diagnosis results which will specify the level of severity (mild, moderate, severe) as well as the areas of infection (high, low)

Detailed Screening using AI

AI basede medical screening technology has the ability to detect more than 13 lungs-diseases like Covid-19, Pneumonia, etc, moreover it continuously learns and adapts itself to your workflow for better accuracy.
